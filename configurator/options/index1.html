Ski Rental Questions, Revised (Part 1)

Size charts:

  • Expanded window is an option
  • Overall ski equipment sizing chart outside of configuration process:
      Skis/Poles: Skier height in feet/inches; skier height in cm >>> suggested ski length, suggested pole length

      Ski boots: Men/Women/Kids: Foot length by cm
    • https://www.evo.com/guides/ski-boot-size-chart-mondo-point-conversion
    • Mondopoint system for Ski boots >>> Comfort Fit for beginners/intermediates, Performance Fit for intermediates/advanced/+s
    • US size, UK size, Euro size
    • https://brannock.com/pages/instructions-fitting-tips >>> Instructions: Measure heel-to-toe length (heel to longest toe, not necessarily first toe)

      Helmets: Head circumference by cm
    • https://www.evo.com/guides/ski-snowboard-helmet-size-fit
    • Instructions: Measure circumference of head just above the eyebrows
    • Kids: XXS (49-50), XS (51-52), S (53-54), M (55-56)
    • Adults: XS (52-53), S (53.5-55.5), M (55.5-57), L (57-59), XL(59-60.5), XXL (60.5-62.5)
    • *User does not need to know "Mondopoint"; will have to try equipment on anyways for quality of fitting once at the ski resort
