Proposal

Ski Equipment Rental

Ideally it will be an interactive version of equipment rental forms one would come across at ski resorts, but with the opportunity to learn a bit about ski equipment and enjoy some mini visuals along the way. Should users like to have rental equipment prepared and ready for pick-up upon their arrival at the resort, they will have to complete the provided questionnaire to customize their order.

Final deliverable: Mobile Clickable Prototype
