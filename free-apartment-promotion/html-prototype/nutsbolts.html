Nuts n' Bolts
  1. This year’s e | z Spring Promotion’s sign-up portal will be open from April 1st until 11:59PM of June 1st.
  2. We will first be drawing for 3 Finalists.
  3. Once entry Finalists are drawn, all entrants will be notified of their status via email.
  4. Finalists will be asked to accept their status and confirm their phone number in the email notification. To remain a Finalist, entrant must respond within 24 hours of receiving the email.
  5. Associated personal will then conduct a brief phone interview to confirm eligibility. (For a list of basic entry qualifications, see below.) Further conditions will differ depending on the apartment location.
  6. A single winner will be selected from the finalist’s pool. If more than one Finalist remains eligible, arbitrary drawing will continue to determine the ultimate Winner.
  7. Winner will be announced on our website and social media. Finalists will be featured as well. For further details, please check our Privacy Policy.
